Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Fried rice is a versatile and flavorful dish that combines cooked rice with a variety of ingredients such as vegetables, proteins (like chicken, shrimp, or tofu), and seasonings. It is typically stir-fried in a wok or large skillet, allowing the flavors to meld together while creating a slightly crispy texture. Fried rice is a popular choice for using up leftover rice and can be customized to suit different tastes and dietary preferences.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • Vegetable oil or sesame oil
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Mixed vegetables (like peas, carrots, and corn)
  • Protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, tofu, etc.)
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce (optional)
  • Eggs (beaten)
  • Green onions (sliced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedures

  1. Heat the oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and stir-fry until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Add the protein of choice and cook until it is fully cooked.
  5. Push the ingredients to the side of the wok and crack the eggs into the center. Scramble the eggs until they are cooked through.
  6. Add the cooked rice to the wok and stir-fry everything together.
  7. Season with soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), salt, and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with sliced green onions before serving.
